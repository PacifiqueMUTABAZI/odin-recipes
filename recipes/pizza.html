PIZZA

Pizza is a beloved and popular dish enjoyed
worldwide. It consists of a round,flat dough
base that is topped with a variety of flavorful
ingredients,typically including cheese,tomato
sauce.


  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoni
  • ½ cup sliced black olives
  • ½ cup sliced bell peppers
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)
